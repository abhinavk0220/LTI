# Insurance Coverage Policy Rules

1. **Coverage Conditions**: The policy covers accidental damage, theft, and third-party liability for private vehicles.
2. **Exclusions**: Damage due to driving under influence (DUI), war, nuclear perils, and mechanical breakdown are strictly excluded.
3. **Max Settlement Limits**: The maximum settlement amount shall not exceed the Insured Declared Value (IDV) of the vehicle minus depreciation.
4. **Document Requirements**: A valid driving license, FIR (for theft/third-party), repair estimate, and policy document are mandatory.
5. **Fraud Indicators**: Claims reported more than 7 days after the incident without valid reason are flagged for high fraud risk.
6. **Depreciation Rules**: 
   - 0-6 months: Nil
   - 6 months - 1 year: 5%
   - 1-2 years: 10%
   - 2-3 years: 15%
   - 3-4 years: 25%
   - 4-5 years: 35%
   - >5 years: 50%
7. **Wait Period**: A cooling-off period of 30 days applies for new policies regarding disease-related claims (not applicable for vehicle accidents, but noted for completeness).
8. **Deductibles**: A compulsory deductible of RS 1000 applies to all claims.
9. **No Claim Bonus**: If no claim is made in the preceding year, a 20% bonus is applied to the renewal premium (not directly affecting claim amount but relevant for policy validity).
10. **Towing Charges**: Towing charges are covered up to RS 1500.
11. **Personal Accident Cover**: Coverage of RS 15,00,000 is included for the owner-driver.
12. **Geographical Limits**: Coverage is valid only within the geographical borders of India.
